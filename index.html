About Benjamin Hemming

Benjamin Hemming, LCSW, LICSW is a dedicated clinical social worker based in South Jordan, Utah, with extensive experience in mental health counseling.

With a PhD in Social Work and multiple licensures, Benjamin provides evidence-based therapeutic approaches to help clients navigate life's challenges and achieve mental wellness.

Currently offering telehealth services only, Benjamin ensures accessible and convenient mental health support for individuals and couples throughout Utah.
